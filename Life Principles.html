Riyaz's Life Principles

Mere Roz ke Rituals — Hosh, Sabr aur Niyat

🧠 1. Hosh (Mindfulness / Awareness)

Main har kaam poori जागरूकता ke saath karta hoon.
👉 Main seat belt laga raha hoon… main paisa sahi jagah use kar raha hoon… main apni cheezein sambhal kar rakh raha hoon.
Mera focus simple hai: behoshi nahi, hosh mein jeena.

⏰ 2. Sabr (Deliberate Delay)

Main turant react nahi karta— pehle rukta hoon, phir decide karta hoon.
👉 Always take a pause while expanding Money, Time an Energy.
Delay to express your emotions
Don't make promise when Riyaz you are happy.

❤️ 3. Niyat (Simple Rule for Growth)

Try to not do bad things. ❌
Try to do good things as much as you can. ✅
